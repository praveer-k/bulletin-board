		Description of the Bulletin Board.
	The information to be displayed on the Bulletin Board
	must follow following Rules :-

1) Approximately 320 characters can be accomodated on this Bulletin board.
   So, any character Beyond that limit may not be visible.

2) The information must be enclosed inside curly braces i.e. '{example, This will be seen on the Bulletin board.}'

3) Any thing writen any where beyond these brackets will not be visible
   on the Bulletin board.

4) The Notice remains on the Bulletin board for Only 10 seconds.

5) The brackets must be present in pairs otherwise The program may behave
   in an un-expected manner.
